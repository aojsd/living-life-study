# Living Life Study - Answers

## Fill in the Blank

### 1. Three definitions of sin (3 pts.)
1. Lawlessness
2. Knowing the good we ought to do and not doing it
3. Everything that doesn't come from faith

### 2. Five components of prayer (5 pts.)
1. Praise
2. Thanksgiving
3. Repentance
4. Intercession
5. Requests

### 3. Books of the Bible (4 pts.)
1. Old Testament: 39 books
2. New Testament: 27 books
3. Old Testament completed: 400 BC
4. New Testament completed: AD 90

### 4. Four categories of Old Testament books (8 pts.)
1. The Law (Genesis to Deuteronomy)
2. History (Joshua to Esther)
3. Wisdom Literature (Job to Song of Songs)
4. Prophets (Isaiah to Malachi)

### 5. Four categories of New Testament books (8 pts.)
1. The Gospels (Matthew to John)
2. History (Acts)
3. Letters (Romans to Jude)
4. Prophetic book (Revelation)

### 6. Three words that describe God (3 pts.)
1. Spirit
2. Light
3. Love

### 7. Triune God in relation to us (3 pts.)
1. God's children
2. Christ's bride
3. The Holy Spirit's temple

### 8. About Jesus Christ (9 pts.)
1. Tribe: Judah
2. Birthplace: Bethlehem
3. Parents: Mary and Joseph
4. Grew up in: Nazareth
5. Profession: Carpenter
6. Age of public ministry: 30 years old
7. Place of death: Golgotha (Calvary)
8. Place of ascension: Mount of Olives
9. Years of public ministry: 3 years

### 9. Israel during Jesus' time (3 pts.)
1. North: Galilee
2. Middle: Samaria
3. South: Judah

### 10. Famous people by time period (3 pts.)
1. BC 2000: Abraham
2. BC 1500: Moses
3. BC 1000: David
4. BC or AD 0: Jesus

### 11. Abraham's descendants and tribes (5 pts.)
- Son: Isaac
- Grandson: Jacob
- Twelve tribes: Reuben, Judah, Levi, Simeon, Benjamin, Ephraim, Manasseh, Zebulun, Gad, Dan, Asher, Naphtali, Issachar

### 12. Spiritual gifts (1 pt.)
Personal answer.
